ABOUT US

» Jack Bloom

My name is Jack Bloom and I'm a creative. I drive the design side of MASS collective; making sure that we are in tune with our brand guide-lines and keeping us with an aesthetic that stays true to the nature of our company. Along with being the creative director for MASS, I also run the design department. Next to design, I also like to cook. Cooking for me is a second passion and always seems to influence my daily life. For me there is a parallel between cooking and design, they both require a keen sense of pairing, timing, and thought.

For me, MASS will be a place to incubate my projects and hopefully an output for great design. I plan on making this organization a place that exhibits the highest quality in branding, packaging, and basically the whole gamut of the graphic design world.
